SQL 语句的执行顺序跟其语句的语法顺序并不一致。SQL 语句的语法顺序是：

-   SELECT[DISTINCT]
-   FROM
-   WHERE
-   GROUP BY
-   HAVING
-   UNION
-   ORDER BY

-   FROM
-   WHERE
-   GROUP BY
-   HAVING
-   SELECT
-   DISTINCT
-   UNION
